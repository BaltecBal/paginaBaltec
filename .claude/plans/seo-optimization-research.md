# SEO Research Findings — Balanceo Baltec

_Permanent audit artifact. Generated: 2026-05-27._

---

## Finding 1: Zero Argentine competitors have meta descriptions

**Summary:** All 10+ competitors crawled (SOMAT, Bayser, Vetrano, BAYRE, DBCTEC, KPS, YAGÜE, Herrametal, Elemak, JPS Argentina) have no visible `<meta name="description">` tag.

**Source:** Direct HTML inspection of competitor sites via browser, 2026-05-27.

**Verdict:** **Confirms** plan. Writing a compelling 155-char meta description for Baltec creates immediate SERP differentiation — competitors show only truncated page text.

---

## Finding 2: Zero Argentine competitors have JSON-LD / schema markup

**Summary:** Not a single industrial balancing competitor in Argentina implements LocalBusiness, Organization, Service, FAQPage, or any other schema type. This is universal across the niche.

**Source:** Direct HTML inspection, 2026-05-27.

**Verdict:** **Confirms and strengthens** plan. Adding LocalBusiness + Service + FAQPage JSON-LD = zero competition for rich results. Google Rich Results (address, hours, star rating) in SERP with no competing signals. Requires no changes to v1 plan.

---

## Finding 3: "Balanceo" dominates over "equilibrado" in Argentine market

**Summary:** Argentine competitors universally use "balanceo dinámico" not "equilibrado dinámico." The term "equilibrado" is the Spanish-Spain variant; Argentine engineers use "balanceo."

**Source:** Frequency analysis across all competitor sites, 2026-05-27.

**Verdict:** **Modifies v1 plan.** Primary keyword must be "balanceo dinámico" throughout. "Equilibrado" should appear as secondary variant (one mention) to capture some cross-border search, but must not replace "balanceo." The backlink research doc listed "equilibrado de maquinaria" as a keyword — this should be deprioritized in v2.

---

## Finding 4: "CABA" and "AMBA" are effective geo-modifiers; no competitor targets zones

**Summary:** Elemak (HVAC competitor) is the only company using "CABA y AMBA" in page title and H1. No industrial balancing competitor targets zone-specific language (zona norte GBA, zona sur GBA, Avellaneda, Pilar, Zárate-Campana corridor).

**Source:** SERP analysis and competitor title tag review, 2026-05-27.

**Verdict:** **Adds to v1 plan.** v2 should include "CABA" and "AMBA" in key metadata and content. Baltec's address (Nueva Pompeya) is in CABA — factually correct. FAQ should include a question about service area covering AMBA. This is a keyword gap no competitor exploits.

---

## Finding 5: "Análisis de vibraciones" and "alineación láser" are searched as companion services

**Summary:** Several competitors (JPS Argentina, YAGÜE, Elemak, Bayser) list vibration analysis and laser alignment as companion services to dynamic balancing. Argentine searchers use "análisis de vibraciones" and "alineación láser" as distinct queries with high commercial intent.

**Source:** Competitor service listing analysis + SERP results for "análisis de vibraciones industrial Buenos Aires", 2026-05-27.

**Verdict:** **Modifies v1 plan.** These keywords must appear in Services.tsx content (Substep 5) not just as service names but in the introductory copy of each service. Also should appear in the site meta description and hero subtitle.

---

## Finding 6: UTA / "unidades manejadoras de aire" is a Baltec-specific competitive gap

**Summary:** No industrial balancing competitor has dedicated content for UTAs. Elemak handles ventilation for UTAs but not balancing/repair. The phrase "balanceo dinámico de unidades manejadoras de aire" has near-zero Argentine content competition.

**Source:** Search for "balanceo UTA Buenos Aires", "balanceo unidades manejadoras de aire Argentina", 2026-05-27.

**Verdict:** **Strengthens v1 plan.** UTA service content in Services.tsx must use the full keyword phrase "Reparación y balanceo dinámico de UTAs (Unidades de Tratamiento de Aire)" — this is the full SEO-friendly form. The service currently uses a shorter label. Also warrants a dedicated FAQ question.

---

## Finding 7: YAGÜE Ingeniería has the most keyword-rich title tag in the sector

**Summary:** YAGÜE's title: "YAGÜE Ingeniería. Especialistas en Dinámica de Máquinas. Vibraciones mecánicas. Strain gauge. Taller de balanceo de rotores. Bahia Blanca" — stacks multiple keywords effectively. They rank for several long-tail terms.

**Source:** Title tag extracted from yagueingenieria.com.ar, 2026-05-27.

**Verdict:** **Informs v1 plan (already captured).** Baltec's title should follow a similar pattern stacking 2–3 keyword phrases. However, avoid pure keyword stuffing — Google has evolved; sentence-like title with embedded keywords outperforms raw stacking. v2 title recommendation: "Balanceo Dinámico Industrial · Reparación de Equipos Rotativos · Baltec Buenos Aires"

---

## Finding 8: ISO 1940 / ISO 21940 are used as trust signals by 5+ competitors

**Summary:** YAGÜE, Bayser, Vetrano, and others reference "norma ISO 1940" or "ISO 21940" (updated standard name) in their content. Engineers and industrial clients search these standards.

**Source:** Competitor content analysis, 2026-05-27. ISO 21940 is the current standard (replaced ISO 1940-1 in 2016); both terms appear in searches.

**Verdict:** **Adds to v1 plan.** Reference to "ISO 21940" (current) + "ISO 1940" (legacy but still searched) should appear in About.tsx copy and in at least one FAQ answer. This is a credibility signal already present in the Hero stats strip ("ISO · Tolerancias mecánicas") but not elaborated in body copy.

---

## Finding 9: Geo-modifier keywords "zona norte GBA", "AMBA industrial" are unclaimed

**Summary:** No competitor targets these geo clusters despite manufacturing plants being distributed across GBA Norte (Pilar, Tigre, Malvinas Argentinas) and GBA Sur (Avellaneda, Lanús, Quilmes, Berazategui) corridors. Baltec's origin was Lanús; current location is CABA (Nueva Pompeya, adjacent to GBA Sur).

**Source:** SERP analysis for "balanceo dinámico zona norte Buenos Aires" and "balanceo equipos rotativos AMBA", 2026-05-27.

**Verdict:** **Adds to v1 plan.** A FAQ question "¿Realizan trabajos en todo el AMBA?" with an answer that naturally names key industrial zones (Avellaneda, Lanús, Quilmes, Pilar, Tigre, Zárate-Campana) gives Baltec geographic keyword coverage no competitor has. Do not create geo-landing pages (deferred) but capture in FAQ + About copy.

---

## Finding 10: Competitor Bayser (bayser.com.ar) appears in top 3 for most primary queries

**Summary:** Bayser ranks consistently alongside Vetrano. Their site is in Luján (GBA Oeste) but targets "Buenos Aires" broadly. Their home page uses a large H1 with "Balanceo y Servicio" — generic. Opportunity: Baltec's more specific CABA location + longer track record is a differentiator.

**Source:** SERP observation across 5 target queries, 2026-05-27.

**Verdict:** **Confirms competitive framing.** Baltec's 40+ years + CABA location + specific client portfolio (Acindar, Banco Nación, Telecom) should be prominent in About copy and be referenced in the meta description for trust-building.
